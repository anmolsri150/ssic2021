SMART SYSTEMS, INNOVATION AND COMPUTING (SSIC-2017)

The conference SSIC 2017 was held on April 14-16, 2017. It brought together a wide range of community focused professionals and industrial experts who have the potential and expertise on the smart systems and Computing. Faculty members, paper presenters, keynote and tutorial participants, students have benefited in many ways from this conference. Total 85 scientific papers out of 327 were presented by authors from India and other countries. Different Invited, Keynote and Tutorial Speakers from different countries addressed to audience and shared knowledge and rich experience of application of Smart Systems in their companies and countries.
  • Multiple Announcements during the event.
