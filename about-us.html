ABOUT CONFERENCE

Smart Systems Innovations in Computing

Smart Systems Innovations in Computing is an international forum for researchers, engineers, academicians as well as industrial professionals from all over the world to share and publicize their research work and development activities in the field of Smart Systems and Intelligent Computing. This conference provides opportunities to different university students to interact with the external researchers and delegates to exchange new ideas and application experiences face to face, also helps the university to find global partners for establishing educational and research relations and for future collaboration.
SSIC will focus on two main areas i.e. Smart Systems and Intelligent Computing. One of the most important technologies that will connect all sectors is intelligent computing. It covers both the theory and applications of the latest computer technologies and methodologies. Providing the state of the art in intelligent methods and techniques for solving real-world problems, along with a vision of future research, the conference will be interesting and valuable for a broad readership. Across sectors energy, power grids, vehicles, homes, entire cities and manufacturing, computers and mobile devices being connected. Though ambitious, there are many challenges in adapting pioneering technologies like smart systems and intelligent computing in the Indian context. Bridging the technology gap, ensuring city administrators are up-to-speed on using the new technologies, the need for a standardized framework and architecture, alignment between technology, businesses and policy making, are a few of them. SSIC will further expand its coverage to include the overlap between theoretical solutions and practical implementations, as well as experimentation and quantitative assessments. This aims to promote translation of basic research into applied investigation and convert applied investigation into the practice. This conference will also create awareness about the importance of basic scientific research in related fields and synchronize with product market.
